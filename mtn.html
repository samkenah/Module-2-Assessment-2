MTN

"Mobile Telephone Networks" redirects here. For cellular radio networks, see cellular network. MTN mobile shop in South Africa MTN Group Limited, formerly M-Cell,[1] is a South African multinational mobile telecommunications company, operating in many African and Asian countries. Its head office is in Johannesburg.[2][3] As of September 2020, MTN recorded 273.44 million subscribers, making it the ninth largest mobile network operator in the world, and the largest in Africa.[4] Active in over 20 countries, one-third of company revenue come from Nigeria, where it holds about 35% market share.[5]
