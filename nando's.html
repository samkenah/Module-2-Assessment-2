NANDO'S

Nando's is a South African fast food chain that specialises in Portuguese-African food, including its signature flame-grilled peri-peri style chicken.[a] Founded in Johannesburg in 1987, Nando's operates over 1,000 outlets in 35 countries. Their logo depicts the Rooster of Barcelos, one of the most common symbols of Portugal.
